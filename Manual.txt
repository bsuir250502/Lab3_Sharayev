1) Specify the number of hospitals(1 <= number_of_hospitals <= 16).
2) Fill information of hospitals in format:
  - Number of beds
  - Coordinates
    - Coordinate X
    - Coordinate Y.
3) Add patients:
  - Specify the surname(set end in this field to stop reading). If this person already in hospital set yes(Y) or no(N) to discharge him of set another surname.
  - Specify coordintes in format:
    - Coordinate X
    - Coordinate Y.
After this steps person will be fitted to the nearest hospital or you will see the message, that there is no empty beds to place him. 
If there is no places, you can discharge someone of the patients and place the another to his bed.
